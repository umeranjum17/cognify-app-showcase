Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Cognify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI assistant application.

By using Cognify, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data via Firebase Auth
  • User Content: Messages and queries you send to the AI assistant
  • Files: Documents and files you upload for AI analysis
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status managed through RevenueCat

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Conversation History: All your chat history is stored locally on your device
  • Usage Statistics: Token usage and costs tracked locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences stored on device

Information Processed Through Third Parties

  • AI Processing: Queries sent to OpenRouter for AI model processing
  • Authentication: Login information processed through Firebase Auth
  • Subscriptions: Payment and subscription data handled by RevenueCat

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our AI assistant services
  • To process your queries and generate AI responses
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize app performance
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Conversation History & Usage Data: All your conversation history, usage statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenRouter: AI queries are processed through OpenRouter's API. Your prompts and responses are transmitted securely but are processed on OpenRouter's servers
  • Firebase Auth: Authentication data is managed by Google Firebase with industry-standard security
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment information is securely handled by RevenueCat

Security Measures

  • Secure HTTPS/TLS connections for all API communications
  • Local encryption for sensitive data on your device
  • Firebase Auth security protocols
  • No storage of conversation data on our servers

Important: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data

Your data is shared only with essential service providers:

  • OpenRouter: Your AI queries are sent to OpenRouter for processing through various AI models
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication data for account management
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

Note: Your conversation history stored on your device is NEVER shared with any third party unless you explicitly export and share it yourself.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data

  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data in the following ways:

In-App Data Deletion

Cognify provides an in-app data deletion feature that allows you to delete all your data directly from within the application:

  • Open the Cognify app on your device
  • Navigate to Settings
  • Scroll down to find "Delete All My Data" option
  • Tap the button and confirm your choice

What gets deleted:

  • All conversation history stored locally on your device
  • Usage statistics and token consumption data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Authentication tokens and API keys
  • Local database files and cached data
  • Your Firebase authentication account (optional)

Email Request

If you cannot access the app or need additional assistance with data deletion, you can email us at:

cognify082025@gmail.com

Please include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line and provide your account details.

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • Local Data: Immediately removed from your device
  • Firebase Auth: Account can be deleted immediately or retained based on your preference
  • Third-Party Services: Data processed by OpenRouter is not permanently stored; RevenueCat subscription data follows their retention policy
  • Backups: Any backup copies will be purged within 30 days

Note: Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have exported any important conversations or data before proceeding with deletion.

Children's Privacy
